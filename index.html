E-cell NITJ is committed on improving today's world and that of the tomorrow through understanding the needs of the hour and helping those who are taking initiative to meet those needs through their Entrepreneurial venture.

What We Do

We at E-Cell NITJ take initiatives to group together various entrepreneurial brains to collaborate, innovate and create solutions for the betterment of society.

Why We Do

E-cell NITJ aspires to develop the required knowledge,skills and spirit of leadership in students to understand the changing world to help, face and solve the enormous obstacles and problems dwelling in the society now and in the coming future, through the Entrepreneurial Spirit.

How We Do

We contact the well established entrepreneurs for inspiration , use the learning to conduct various events for the growth of curious entrepreneur brains at our institute.
